McCormick's Creek

At this location we hike along two trails where we see accessible caves and waterfalls!

Cedar Bluffs

This hike is closer to a mile long and has a fun spot to stop and skip rocks as well as a scenic creek to walk along. It also has an amazing view at the top of the bluff.

Green's Bluff

This hike features a pretty creek where we often stop to skip rocks and a gorgeous overlook near the end of the two mile hike.

Leonard Springs

This hike offers some cave openings you can crawl in as well as nice calm walk through the woods.

Brown County

A place we often go in the Fall to hike around Ogle lake while the leaves change colors. We also stop to see the many beautiful overlooks at this State Park.

Patton Cave

A four mile hike that sometimes can take a little longer depending on the route. It leads to a massive Cave entrance and overall is the most challenging hike we do.

Dilcher Turner Canyon Forest

The location here features 2.5 miles of trails with some awesome views and a cool waterfall!.
