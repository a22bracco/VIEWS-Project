What We Do?

Views at IU is the premier hiking club at Indina University. The club was founded by Giulio Bracco who wanted to hike with the focus of finding the best views in and around Bloomington. Since the founding of the club in 2018 it has grown to over 200 members. We hike 1-2 times a week usually on the weekends. We also do multiple social events throughout the semester as well as one camping trip per semester. We have gone to awesome places like the Red River Gorge and Mammoth Cave.

We strive to provide IU students with the opportunity to explore the outdoors and all they have to offer.

Why Join Us?

We are a fun club that seeks the adventure of the outdoors and every fun opportunity it has to offer. We are a welcoming that wants any and everyone interested to join us and experience the fun of our club.
