How We Started

Our Founder, Giulio Bracco came to college as an avid hiker and in his second year IU began looking for a group to enjoy this interesst with others. Giulio saw the "Hiking Club at IU" to only find that they hiked for mileage. This was not his goal when hiking so he gathered some of his friends and founded "Views at IU" with the focus of finding the best views in and around Bloomington.
